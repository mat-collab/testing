What We Do

Everything Your Luggage Needs

Whether you're running an international airport or a boutique hotel, we have a service tier built for your operation.

Curbside Check-In

Guests drop off bags at the entrance. Our agents tag, weigh, and deliver them directly to the aircraft or hotel room — no queuing required.

Door-to-Door Delivery

We collect luggage from home and deliver it to the destination — arriving before your guest does, every time.

Secure Storage

Climate-controlled, CCTV-monitored storage facilities at major hubs. Short-term lockers to long-term warehouse solutions.

Real-Time Tracking

Every bag gets a smart RFID tag and GPS milestone updates via our app or your existing PMS integration.

Hotel Concierge Program

White-label luggage handling under your hotel brand — from check-in to in-room delivery and express checkout pickups.

Fleet Transfer

Inter-terminal and inter-hotel transfers handled by our dedicated fleet of tracked, insured vehicles operating 24/7.
